How to Collect Data
DataWinners allows you to collect your data in 3 different ways. Choose the channels most convenient for you and your Data Senders.

  1. Web
    Collect an unlimited amount of data using the online Questionnaire.
  2. Android Smartphone
    Download the Questionnaires directly onto your Android Smartphone.
  3. SMS
    Use any simple mobile phone to send in data using a single SMS.
    See which telephone number you can use to send in your data.For the free trial account, International SMS rates apply. You pay to send your messages to DataWinners. DataWinners will send automatic confirmation and error messages back in response to your SMS. You can submit up to 50 messages during your trial period.
