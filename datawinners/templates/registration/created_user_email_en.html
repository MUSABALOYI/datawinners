How to Create Project
On the Dashboard page, click on «Create a new Project» to create your Questionnaire. Once you have created your project, you can register all the people who send you data (we call them Data Senders).

How to Collect Data
DataWinners allows you to collect your data in 3 diffrent ways, Choose the channel most convenient for you and your Data Senders.

  1. Web: Collect an unlimited amount of data using the online Questionnaire.
  2. Android Smartphone: Download the Questionnaires directly onto your Android Smartphone.
  3. SMS: Use any simple mobile phone to send data using a single SMS. Send your SMS to the telephone number {{ org_number }}. Datawinners will send automatic confirmation and error messages back in response to your SMS.
