Export Your Data
If you do not plan to upgrade your account, you can export your collected data by following these steps:
1. Log into your DataWinners account at www.datawinners.com
2. Choose one of your DataWinners projects
3. Go to the "Data" tab
4. Go to the "Submissions Log" tab
5. Choose “Export” in the top-right corner
6. Repeat this process for each of your data collection projects
